About Me

Senior Ruby on Rails developer and GIS/automation specialist, available fractionally or as a freelancer — experienced, independent help without agency overhead.

Specialising in the harder end of Rails work: legacy upgrades, long-running system stability, deployment and ops, and the operational problems that don't have obvious answers. A particular niche in geospatial systems: map platforms, planning and property portals, FME/Rails APIs, CAD/web integrations and geospatial automation.

Need experienced Rails help without hiring full-time? Get in touch.
